Welcome to the Sudoku Solver!

This is a program that allows you to enter in your own Sudoku puzzle and solves it for you.

Simply move your mouse over a space and you can type in any number from 1 through 9.

If you're unfamiliar with Sudoku, a valid solution is one where every row, column, and 3x3 box has only one occurrence of every number 1 through 9. There can be no duplicate numbers in any of them.

If you enter in a duplicate number in a row, box, or column, that space will turn red as well as any spaces with the duplicate number. The program will not solve it until all spaces are white.

Simply click the Solve button when ready or the Clear button to reset the board.
